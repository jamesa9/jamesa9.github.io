 Social Media in Psychology
Site Author
 Helen Santoro
Abstract
 This article gives insight on how social media is being used as a form of technology in psychological research studies. It provides information on what psychologist’s plans are for using social media platforms to spread awareness about mental health. It, also, explains why they are trying to diminish the depiction of what a typical psychologist looks and act like. They want the public to know more about mental disorders and how they can be overlooked because they are undetectable. They believe this is a great way to get the generation’s attention on how to access resources and learn methods that can help with managing with their conditions.

 Gaming in Psychology
Site Author
 Deborah Bach
Abstract
 This article explains the many different ways gaming has played a role in mental health and psychology. It provides insight on how different people used gaming to cope with their anxiety, depression, or any other disorders. It eliminates all the harmful stereotypes that are associated with gaming and how it is actually beneficial in helping people becoming more comfortable with their mental health. It also explains how people have decided to incorporate characters dealing with mental health in certain games. That way the mental health community has someone who represents them and what their struggles are like in everyday life.
