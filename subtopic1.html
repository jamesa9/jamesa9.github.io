Abstract
 This article gives a depth information about what Electroencephalogram, or EEG, is and how it is used in the medical field. It describes how it is uses instruments attached to the head that collect brain waves. This is then collected and used to inform doctors and patients the type of diagnoses the person has. This website also provides information on the type of conditions it is used for and how they are determined. It informs readers how to prepare for the EEG and how the test is going to go. Any questions that you have about what an EEG is and how it functions this website clears it up for you!

Abstract
 This website gives great information about what a Magnetic resonance imaging, or an MRI, is and why it is needed in the medical field. It is a huge instrument shaped like a long, cylinder tube and it creates precise images of the human body’s insides. This machine is also helpful to identify what health condition is in the body and where it’s located. Not only does it determine the condition, but it also helps patients track if their treatment is going well for their condition. The site also provides information on how the machine operates and what occurs during the MRI scan. It explains how to be safe when getting the scan and what parts of the body the MRI scan is scanning.
